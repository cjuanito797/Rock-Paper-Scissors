Please Make Sure You Understand The Following Rules:

1. Make sure that your camera is turned on.

2. Tap play, there will be a camera that will display your current hand gesture (Rock, Paper or Scissor)

3. Any other hand gesture that isn't one of the above are marked as being inappropriate hand gesture.

4. Once you are ready, press the Q button and a timer will start a countdown and whatever hand-gesture the camera stopped at will be your choice.

5. The CPUs choice will be randomly generated and compare the two choices. Paper beat Rock, Rock beat Scissors and Scissors beat Paper.
